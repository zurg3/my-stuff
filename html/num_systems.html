Numeral systems converter

Hints

You can press hotkeys to switch between input fields:

  • Alt+B - BIN
  • Alt+O - OCT
  • Alt+1 - DEC
  • Alt+H - HEX

Allowed input:

  • BIN: 0-1
  • OCT: 0-7
  • DEC: 0-9
  • HEX: 0-F

HEX letter symbols:

  • A = 10
  • B = 11
  • C = 12
  • D = 13
  • E = 14
  • F = 15
